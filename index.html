Context

I'm Madelyn Lewis, interior architecture and design student at Bob Jones University. This website is a collection of projects I've completed over the past few years that cover a range of fields including residential, corporate, healthcare, and more. Changing a space to be more functionally and aesthetically pleasing to both client + user is what I love to do, and I hope that comes through in my projects. Happy scrolling!
